Who Are Hackers?

  • Hackers are skilled individuals who explore and manipulate computer systems.
  • They possess deep knowledge of programming and problem-solving.
  • Hackers can be categorized as ethical (white-hat) or malicious (black-hat).
  • Ethical hackers help secure systems by identifying vulnerabilities.
  • Black-hat hackers exploit systems for personal or financial gain.
  • Gray-hat hackers operate in a mix of ethical and unethical practices.
  • Hackers play a significant role in the evolution of cybersecurity.
  • They are often portrayed in media as both heroes and villains.
